Delete Account

At LovZa, we respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you generally have two options:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your account.

  1. Open the LovZa app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon usually found in the profile tab).
  3. Scroll down to the bottom and tap on Delete Account.
  4. Follow the on-screen confirmation steps.
  5. Your account and data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, you can contact our support team.

  • Email: support@lovza.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Please include your registered phone number or email address so we can locate your account.

Please note that manual deletion requests may take up to 30 days to process.

Data Retention

Once your account is deleted, your profile will no longer be visible to other users. We may retain certain data for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business validation purposes (e.g., fraud prevention), as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
